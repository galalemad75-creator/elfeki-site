About Dr. Ibrahim El-Feki

Dr. Ibrahim El-Feki is a renowned motivational speaker, author, and life coach dedicated to helping individuals unlock their full potential through the power of positive thinking and personal development.

With decades of experience in psychology and human behavior, Dr. El-Feki has touched the lives of millions through his books, seminars, and now — this podcast.

About This Podcast

This podcast brings you carefully curated episodes covering topics like:

  • Positive thinking and mental resilience
  • Time management and productivity
  • Self-improvement and personal growth
  • Leadership and success principles
  • Health, wellness, and mindfulness
  • Building meaningful relationships

Each episode is designed to inspire, educate, and empower you on your journey to becoming the best version of yourself.
